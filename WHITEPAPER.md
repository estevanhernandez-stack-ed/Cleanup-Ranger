## 🌍 The Problem: Ecological State Drift

Across the globe, public parks and natural reserves are facing a silent crisis. We define this as **Ecological State Drift**: the gradual, often invisible degradation of public land integrity due to neglect, litter, or structural damage.

Current reporting tools are:

- **Reactive, not proactive**: Reports take weeks to process.
- **Fragmented**: Local governments operate in silos.
- **Unverified**: There is no closed-loop system to prove a fix was effective.

## 📡 The Solution: Cleanup Ranger

Cleanup Ranger is a high-performance OPS (Operations) platform designed to synchronize volunteers and AI agents into a single, resilient stewardship network.

### 🛡️ Core Capabilities

#### 1. The Command Radar (Real-time Telemetry)

A high-fidelity map interface providing a real-time heat map of sector health. Every park "Node" displays its current status, active hazards, and the squad currently assigned to its protection.

#### 2. The Drift Scanner (Gemini 3.1 AI)

Using **Gemini 3 Flash**, users can scan hazards in the field. The AI doesn't just "see" an image; it classifies the ecological impact, determines the required equipment (Smart Manifests), and assigns a priority level based on potential "state drift" acceleration.

#### 3. Impact Verification (Proof of Remediation)

To prevent fraud and ensure quality, Cleanup Ranger utilizes a secondary AI pass. Volunteers must scan the site after cleanup; Gemini 3.1 Pro compares the "Before" and "After" states to verify remediation, only then awarding **Legacy Points**.

#### 4. Squad Operations & Leaderboards

Stewardship is gamified through a sophisticated points and badge system. Users join "Squads" to dominate sector leaderboards, turning environmental protection into a community-driven competition.
