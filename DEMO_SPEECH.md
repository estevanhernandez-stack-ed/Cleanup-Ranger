# 🎤 Cleanup Ranger: Demo Video Speech Script

This script is designed for a **2-3 minute** walkthrough of the **Cleanup Ranger** platform.

---

## 🕒 [0:00 - 0:20] Hook & Mission

- **Speech**: "Welcome to Cleanup Ranger—the decentralised stewardship platform built for the future of environmental management. We merge autonomous agent intelligence with community action to protect our green commons."
- **Visuals**: Landing page hero section, smooth scroll.

---

## 🗺️ [0:20 - 0:50] The Radar (Map View)

- **Speech**: "Our primary interface is the Command Radar. Using the Google Places API, we dynamically track the health of every park and territory in your area in real-time."
- **Action**: Pan and zoom on the map. Show the "Scanning" badge.
- **Speech**: "As you move, the system discovers new territories, building a live database of stewardship opportunities without duplication."
- **Visuals**: Sidebar filtering parks by viewport (the new sync feature!).

---

## 🛰️ [0:50 - 1:20] AI Reporting (Drift Scanner)

- **Speech**: "When a 'State Drift' is detected—like litter or damage—our Drift Scanner uses Google Gemini to classify the issue instantly."
- **Action**: Click a park, open "Report Hazard." Upload a photo (use one from `/e2e/fixtures`).
- **Speech**: "The AI generates a smart manifest of required tools and describes the impact, turning raw data into a mission-ready cleanup rally."

---

## ✅ [1:20 - 1:45] Cleanup Verification

- **Speech**: "But stewardship requires proof. After a cleanup, users submit work for verification."
- **Action**: Open "Verify Cleanup."
- **Speech**: "Our secondary AI pass verifies that the territory has regained its integrity points before rewarding the squad. This ensures high-trust, impact-driven results."

---

## 🛡️ [1:45 - 2:15] Squads & Achievements

- **Speech**: "It's all about the community. Our Squad System allows users to form specialized units, and track their stats and rankings locally."
- **Action**: Open "Manage Squad" modal. Toggle between Members and Stats.
- **Speech**: "Points, badges, and territory ranks turn civic duty into a shared, competitive mission."

---

## 🚀 [2:15 - 2:30] The Future (Roadmap)

- **Speech**: "And we’re just getting started. Check out our roadmap to see the upcoming integration of IoT sensors, drone recon swarms, and business bounties."
- **Action**: Quickly show the About/Roadmap page.
- **Speech**: "Join the Rangers. Protect the wild. Stand ready."

---

## 📋 Pro Tips for the Recording:

- Use a **high-framerate** screen recorder (60fps).
- **Hide the browser toolbar** (F11) for a premium "app" feel.
- Ensure the **Spotlight Tour** button is visible in the corner for judges to see.
